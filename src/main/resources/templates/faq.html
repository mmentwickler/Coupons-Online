How do I pay the voucher ?

Card payment is done safely via Paypal. Card data is securely sent directly to Paypal without being in possession of Pikupons.com Once the payment has been completed, you will be redirected back to our site. You will not pay any additional charge for the card payment. It does not matter what type of card you have, national or international, Visa, MasterCard or Maestro - we accept all of them! Paying with the Card pay attention to the amount required to pay! If you are only required to pay the voucher, pay only the voucher, and the discount amount will be paid out! Do not pay the full amount of the offer, because the full amount must be paid locally to the bidder / local / Company listed in the Pikupons offer! For example: "Get your: 1$ Pikupons voucher and pay only 10$ instead of 20$" - in this case you only have to pay with the Card 1$ - the voucher price and the remaining 10$ You'll pay it back to the Trader.

If the payment process is an error, the main reason would be the lack of funds on your card. Check your card balance and try again!.
